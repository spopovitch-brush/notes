## Dark Matter Project

### 10 Steps
1. Lead with the *wow*, not the theory 
2. Explain the why, not just the what
3. Keep it short, but not shallow (never simplify)
4. Show your face, and your fascination
5. Frame it as a story (evoking curiosity)
6. Design for silent viewing (subtitle)
7. Connect to everyday life
8. Vertical, mobile-first viewing
9. End with an invitation
10. Post regularly, test relentlessly
